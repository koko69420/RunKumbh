About the Event

The RunKumbh Marathon is an initiative by the NCC cadets of RVITM College to promote fitness and community spirit among RVITM students and JP Nagar residents.

This event is open exclusively to RVITM students and JP Nagar residents. All participants will receive an event T-shirt, refreshments, participation medal, and physical certificate.
