About the Event

The RunKumbh Marathon is an initiative by the NCC cadets of RVITM College to promote fitness, discipline, and community spirit among students and the general public.

This year's marathon will feature race categories for both men and women. All proceeds will go towards supporting NCC activities and social causes.
